Privacy Policy for Podbbang Unofficial

Last updated: August 22, 2025

Information We Collect

Podbbang Unofficial is an unofficial client for accessing Podbbang's podcast content. This application:

  • Does not collect personal information from users
  • Does not store user data locally beyond app preferences
  • Accesses Podbbang's public API to retrieve podcast content

Data Usage

This application:

  • Retrieves podcast information from Podbbang's public API
  • Stores user preferences locally on your device
  • Does not transmit personal data to third parties

Third-Party Services

This app accesses Podbbang's services to retrieve podcast content. Please refer to Podbbang's privacy policy for information about their data practices.
